Wrath of Olympus Team Registration

Event Description

Welcome to the Wrath of Olympus Team registration page! Join our elite crew of fishers to complete layers, help people hunt and fight the exotics (and maybe the devil). Please ensure your details are accurate before submitting.

Rods and Totems

For Disturber, it is recommended to use rods with high progress speed and high disturbance stat while enchanted with ferocious and vicious. (e.g.: Plaguereaver)

For Totems, this include:

  • Aurora
  • Eclipse (for Sunshot)
  • Poseidon's Wrath (for Tridentfish)
  • Zeus' Storm (for Charged Water)
  • Starfall (optional, for progress speed boost)

If you dont have one of them, its okay, you can just buy them anytime when needed.

Hunting

Because most of hunt fishes are single-hook only, At first time everyone can join the hunt, but the person who caught the hunt fish should not join the next same hunt. For Olympian Godbreaker, one person must catch the Olympian Devil in order to reveal the portal. (Requires full olympus bestiary and 50M C$)
